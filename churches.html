Use Famplify as a creative new approach to building your family-focused platform

- It's Fun! -

Groups provide meaningful family fellowship!

Streaming this content into your venue will create an environment that will teach how to conduct a family Bible study while also providing a great time of fellowship.

* This social platform provides a PERFECT opportunity for the families in your group to invite OTHER FAMILIES IN THEIR COMMUNITIES to YOUR church or home group!

- It's Easy! -

Sign up is free and sending invites is easy!

Sign-up a group for free and then invite as many families to participate in your “fellowship night” as you’d like.

* Families with an account can then request to be apart of your group. Your approval gives them access to all the material you purchase at a discounted bulk rate!

- It's Affordable! -

Buying in bulk has always had its benefits!

One Group Credit is $120.00
A Group = 15 Family Credits
That is a 60 dollar savings!

* A group 15 families large means 5 FAMILIES are getting it for FREE!!!
